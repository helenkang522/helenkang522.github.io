Introduction

KickStop

KickStop is your local store where you can pick up the latest streetwear fashion and connect with other local sneakerheads to trade and link with.

Target audience

We cater to the streetwear fashion community who love to express themselves through their clothing and shoes.

Our goal is to strengthen the community of like minded people to help each other snag the next pair of sneakers they've been eyeing and connect with others to keep everyone in the loop with upcoming clothing drops and such.
